Roundtable Proof-of-Human offers flexible pricing plans designed to scale with your needs. Start with our **Starter** plan and upgrade to **Enterprise** as your requirements grow.

## Starter


- 14-day free trial
- $99/month base price
- Includes 100,000 sessions per month
- Additional sessions at $0.50 per 1,000 sessions
